A bigger Housemait update is out.

Version 1.4.0 is live in the App Store. Here's what's new.

👨‍👩‍👦 Share tasks and events properly. Assign a task or event to multiple family members at once. When one of you ticks it off, it's done for everyone — no more "did you do the eye drops or do I need to?"

🏫 School term dates, on tap. Paste your school's term-dates link (or upload the PDF) and Housemait imports terms, half-terms, and INSET days automatically. Works for multiple schools per household.

☀️ A friendlier morning brief. Your 7 AM WhatsApp digest now leads with the weather, today's dinner from your meal plan, and anything due today or tomorrow — all in one quick scan.

⏰ Custom reminder timings that actually work. Ask the bot for a 20-minute or 45-minute reminder and you'll get it — no more silent rounding to "5 minutes."

📄 Send PDFs in the chat. Drop in a school newsletter or party invite — the AI pulls out the dates and adds them to your calendar.

Plus dozens of small reliability fixes — recurring tasks no longer duplicate themselves, the bot no longer claims to have added things it didn't, and the weather knows where you live.
