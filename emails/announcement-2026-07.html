Chores & routines

Chores kids actually want to do

Morning and evening routines and one-off chores, each worth a few stars. Kids tick them off and watch their streak grow. No nagging required. Everyone sees who’s done what, in real time.

Rewards

Stars they can spend on what matters

Turn earned stars into rewards you choose. 30 minutes of screen time, a later bedtime, a trip to the zoo, or in-app stickers and themes. Kids see exactly how close they are, and redeem when they’re ready.

To-dos and the shopping list, together and shared with the whole house. Everything sorts itself into Today, This week and Someday, so the important things never get buried.

Finally a good reason to give them a screen. Build healthy habits with Child Mode. A bright, playful world with their quests, stars and event countdowns — and nothing else. No ads, no strangers, no grown-up clutter. You decide what they see.
